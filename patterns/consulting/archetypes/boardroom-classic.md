# Boardroom Classic

The default consulting style: a calm, dense, evidence-forward recommendation deck
that earns trust by showing the work.

**Best for:** strategy recommendations, value cases, market sizing, M&A logic.
**Visual language:** mostly white background, restrained layout, dense but orderly
evidence, disciplined footnotes and source lines, a single blue/grey accent.
Charts and tables take priority over decoration.

## Rules

- Action titles on every slide; the titles read as one continuous argument.
- One dominant exhibit per slide, with a one-line so-what.
- Strong footnote and `Source:` discipline.
- Quiet ink: ~90% neutral, one accent doing all the pointing.
- Prefer native tables and charts over drawn graphics.

## Pattern emphasis

C05 action-title evidence · C06 single chart · C08 waterfall · C10 KPI row ·
C12 2×2 · C13 issue tree · C15 option comparison · C16 scorecard ·
C24 business case · C30 recommendation.

## Note

A neutral archetype, inspired by the *grammar* of public analytical decks. It
copies no firm's template, palette, logo, or wording.
