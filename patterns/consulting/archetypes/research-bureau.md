# Research Bureau

A report you can present: chaptered, survey-driven, with a clear methodology and
full-bleed section dividers.

**Best for:** public research, survey decks, institute and thought-leadership
reports.
**Visual language:** chapter navigation, full-bleed section dividers carrying a
large numeral, an executive summary, a methodology page, survey small multiples,
more report-like structure than a client recommendation.

## Rules

- Chaptered structure with clear section dividers.
- Open with an executive summary; include methodology and sources.
- Survey results as small multiples and segmented comparisons.
- Respondent counts and bases stated near the data.
- Reads as a publication, not a pitch.

## Pattern emphasis

C02 executive summary · C04 section divider · C07 small multiples · C11 benchmark
ranking · C16 scorecard · C28 segment comparison · C32 methodology ·
C33 assumptions.

## Note

A neutral archetype, inspired by the *grammar* of public research reports. It
copies no firm's template, palette, logo, or wording.
