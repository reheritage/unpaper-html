# Operator Redline

Direct, pragmatic, commercial. A strong top rule, a sparing red accent, and
ranked charts that get to the point.

**Best for:** commercial strategy, market studies, pragmatic recommendations.
**Visual language:** a strong horizontal top rule, red accent used sparingly for
emphasis, simple ranked bar charts and rankings, a clear source/confidentiality
footer strip.

## Rules

- Blunt action titles — the finding, stated plainly.
- A single strong rule or accent line anchors the page.
- Red marks the one thing that matters on a slide, nothing else.
- Simple charts and ranked lists over elaborate frameworks.
- A consistent source / confidentiality footer.

## Pattern emphasis

C05 action-title evidence · C06 single chart · C08 waterfall · C11 benchmark
ranking · C15 option comparison · C23 initiative portfolio · C28 segment
comparison · C30 recommendation.

## Note

A neutral archetype, inspired by the *grammar* of public pragmatic market-study
decks. It copies no firm's template, palette, logo, or wording.
