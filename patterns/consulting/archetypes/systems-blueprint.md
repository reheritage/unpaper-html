# Systems Blueprint

The implementation style: modular boxes, swimlanes, capability maps and roadmaps.
For decks about *how the work gets done*.

**Best for:** transformation programmes, operating models, PMO, implementation
plans.
**Visual language:** modular boxes, process and swimlane diagrams, capability
maps, more tables and diagrams than charts, strong section labels.

## Rules

- Structure the content as systems: capabilities, roles, interfaces, phases.
- Use diagrams that mean something (a flow for a sequence, swimlanes for
  parallel workstreams, an org/operating model for accountability).
- Strong, consistent section labels and numbering.
- Tables for workplans, owners and dependencies.

## Pattern emphasis

C17 maturity ladder · C18 roadmap · C19 workplan/Gantt-lite · C20 process flow ·
C21 operating model · C22 org/governance · C23 initiative portfolio · C25 risk &
mitigation · C27 customer journey.

## Note

A neutral archetype, inspired by the *grammar* of public transformation decks. It
copies no firm's template, palette, logo, or wording.
