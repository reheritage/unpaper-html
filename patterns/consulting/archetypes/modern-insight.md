# Modern Insight

An open, modern readout: fewer objects per slide, generous whitespace, clean
charts. Confidence through clarity rather than density.

**Best for:** research readouts, thought leadership, executive insight decks.
**Visual language:** open whitespace, simple bar/line charts, one confident accent
colour, few or no boxes, frequent small multiples.

## Rules

- One idea per slide, given room to breathe.
- Simple charts with sparse labels (labels inside or beside bars, not a legend
  the eye has to hunt for).
- Small multiples to compare segments or periods on one shared scale.
- One accent, used decisively and rarely.

## Pattern emphasis

C02 executive summary · C06 single chart · C07 small multiples · C10 KPI row ·
C12 2×2 · C18 roadmap · C29 case example · C32 methodology.

## Note

A neutral archetype, inspired by the *grammar* of public modern research slides.
It copies no firm's template, palette, logo, or wording.
